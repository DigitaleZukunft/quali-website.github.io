FAQ

What is token‑gating and why is it better?

Token‑gating uses verifiable ownership to grant access, stopping bots and low‑effort spam. It keeps signal high and protects your members’ time.

Which tokens and chains are supported?

We support major chains and token standards including NFTs and fungible tokens. Multi‑chain means your access follows you.

Do you store my data?

No. We keep your rooms private and encrypted. We never sell or share your data.

How fast can I launch?

Minutes. Connect your wallet, set your rules, and you’re live—already in spaces for tokens you hold.

Is there a web client?

Yes. You can use the web login to access chats from desktop and mobile browsers.

How are tokens verified?

We read directly from chain RPCs or indexers and cache proofs securely. No custody of your assets.

What wallets are supported?

We aim to support major wallets on each chain. If your favorite is missing, tell us.

Can I invite non‑holders?

Yes. Time‑boxed guest passes and event tickets are planned. Request early access.
